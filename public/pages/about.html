About the Computer Science Club
Our Mission

As Cy-Fair's computer science club, we are dedicated to preparing for the Computer Science UIL Competition and Codewars. We go to mock UIL competitions that are held locally. In order to compete, each member will be placed in a team of three. These teams should stay together throughout the year and go to as many competitions as they can.

Joining the Club

If you have no coding experience, fear not! Prior programming experience is not required to join the club nor is being in a Cy-Fair Computer Science class. Come to the club, so we can find a place for you!
